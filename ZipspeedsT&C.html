5. Driver Partner Responsibilities

As a Driver Partner, you agree to:

Vehicle Maintenance: Ensure your vehicle is in good working condition and compliant with applicable laws and regulations.

Adherence to Safety Guidelines: Follow all road safety rules, wear appropriate safety gear, and drive responsibly.

Handling of Goods: Transport goods carefully to avoid damage or loss. Any damage caused due to negligence will be the responsibility of the driver.

Communication: Keep customers and Zipspeeds updated about delivery statuses and report any delays or issues promptly.

Compliance: Follow all applicable local laws, including traffic laws, environmental regulations, and vehicle standards.

Payment: Make timely payment for services provided, as outlined by Zipspeeds. Any late payments may result in penalties or suspension of services.

Package Readiness: Ensure that items for delivery are securely packed and ready for pickup at the scheduled time.
